## рҹҺ® Game Story

**Year 4089.** Earth has nearly been destroyed. Only 3 continents remain:
- **Faultmine** вҖ” Cracked earth, toxic mines, industrial ruins
- **Wrapzones** вҖ” Warped reality, glitchy terrain
- **Akustus Kisemo** вҖ” Dark, mysterious, deep unknown territory

Two soldiers вҖ” **Naveen** and **Radhika** вҖ” emerge from the darkness to discover robots have replaced and enslaved all humans. They meet **Jenny**, a survivor who gives them their first weapon and reveals where the people are held.

**Goal:** Free the enslaved humans by destroying mindless robots across all 5 levels.
